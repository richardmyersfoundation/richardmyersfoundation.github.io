Mission Statement

The Richard Myers Foundation Inc. (RMF) is committed to improve the health and wellness of underprivileged, distressed, and impoverished communities.



Vision
Statement

We strive to provide academic and financial access, tools, and wellness opportunities to enhance the mental, social, and economic status of underprivileged families by providing essential basic needs and opportunities for lifelong learning.
